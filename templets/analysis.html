Analysis

Description

A survey was done among the 2022 batch students from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi in order to better understand the parameters impacting student success in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE). The extensive survey encompassed a wide range of criteria in order to obtain insight into the students' preparation, background, and motivation, with the ultimate goal of predicting their JEE Advanced scores. The survey covered a variety of crucial characteristics, beginning with the amount of time students devote to both school and JEE preparation. This key feature reveals the students' effort and commitment to striking a balance between their normal studies and hard JEE preparation. We also examined the educational quality of the student's parents, which could influence the academic environment and support at home.
