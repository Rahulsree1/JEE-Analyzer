Analysis

Time spend on Digital media

Observation-

Most of the students spent time 2-3 hours per day on digital media which includes mobile phone, T .V, or other entertainment sources.
This is also important as it refreshes the mind. But the student should not become addicted to it. In 12th this time gradually decreases.

Physical activities VS Marks

Observation-

Physical Activities are also very important aspect in student’s life as it reduces student’s stress, improves his concentration and enhances memory retention.
It also improves the focus. So many students spend some time (less than 2 hours) in some kind of physical activities like sports, yoga , exercises etc. But In class
12th this time decreases as they have more pressure of study in 12th .
